# PlantOS Product Overview

## 1. Vision
PlantOS brings intelligence, structure, and community to plant care.  
It’s built for hobbyists, collectors, and growers who want to organize their plant care and connect with others.  

PlantOS transforms plant care into a continuous loop: **observe → act → learn → optimize.**  
AI assistance and data tracking turn growing into both an art and a science.

---

## 2. The Problem
Plant owners struggle with inconsistent schedules, scattered data, and one-size-fits-all advice.  
PlantOS unifies care automation, AI insights, propagation management, and a thriving plant marketplace.

---

## 3. The Solution
PlantOS:
1. Identifies plants using photos.  
2. Builds optimized care schedules.  
3. Diagnoses plant health automatically.  
4. Tracks propagation and growth.  
5. Lets users experiment and share results.  
6. Enables sales and trading between growers.  

---

## 4. User Experience
### 4.1 Plant Management
- Add a plant → AI identifies species & creates care plan  
- Syncs watering & feeding schedules  
- Displays a timeline of growth and events

### 4.2 AI Assistance
- Explains issues like yellow leaves or drooping  
- Suggests actions based on conditions  
- Answers care questions naturally

### 4.3 Experiments
- Users A/B test watering or fertilizer strategies  
- Logs outcomes automatically with photos  
- Compares AI models (Gemini, Claude, GPT) for effectiveness

### 4.4 Propagation & Inventory
- Tracks mother plants, propagation batches, and growth stages  
- Converts ready cuttings into sellable inventory  
- Links propagation data to marketplace listings

### 4.5 Marketplace
- Peer-to-peer plant sales and trades  
- Secure Stripe payments & commissions  
- AI pricing, listing descriptions, and “fit with your home” suggestions

---

## 5. Data & AI Philosophy
- Local-first privacy; user data remains accessible offline  
- Aggregated, anonymized insights fuel collective learning  
- Modular LLM architecture allows experimentation across providers

---

## 6. Monetization
- Platform fee (10–15%) on marketplace transactions  
- Premium tiers for advanced analytics and commercial tools  
- Potential partnerships with nurseries and suppliers  

---

## 7. Roadmap & Future
- IoT sensor integration (humidity, temperature)  
- Open API for third-party apps  
- AI model training based on anonymized data  
- Expansion into global plant-trade and education network

---

## 8. Ethos
PlantOS merges precision and empathy — technology that helps people grow living things, thoughtfully and sustainably.
